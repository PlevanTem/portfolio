NightRaven

We aim to make women feel more safe when walking alone, by ensuring the ability of calling for help even though the women may not be able to dial themselves.


Problems


  • Women feeling scared when walking alone at night.
  • Not able to contact police if they are attacked.
  • Even if police is called, the assault may still happen, because perpretator does not know the police have been contacted.


Solutions
